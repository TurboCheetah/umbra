Umbra

E2E encrypted chat

When you join a room, you are given a public key and a private key. When your partner joins your room your public keys are exchanged automatically. Whenever you send a message, it is encrypted using your partner's public key. This means it can only be decrypted using the corresponding private key. When your partner sends a message, it is encrypted using your public key. When you recieve it, the message is decrypted with your private key. This ensures no third-parties have access to the messages sent. All anyone (besides whoever has the private key) can see is a scrambled and undecipherable mess. We can't even decrypt your message as we don't have you or your partner's private keys!
